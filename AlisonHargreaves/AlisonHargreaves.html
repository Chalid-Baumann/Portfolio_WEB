ALISON HARGREAVES

Infographics,
Illustration,
Prototyping,
Animation

As part of the Infographics module during my bachelor's degree, I developed an infographic portrait of influential female mountaineers, focusing on the British climber Alison Hargreaves. This project includes two parts, a booklet and a touch screen that will be part of an exhibition in the museum.
