Hair curler manual

Illustration

In this bachelor degree module, the goal was to interpret the toilet roll in a new way and design a manual with pictograms. By sketching I found a practical, yet innovative and sustainable idea. This manual shows how to use a toilet roll as a hair curler. Normally it just gets thrown away and disgregarded instantly. However in this way I create a new perspective as well as load the object with more importance.
