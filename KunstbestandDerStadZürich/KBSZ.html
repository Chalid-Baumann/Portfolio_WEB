Kunstbestand der Stadt Zürich

Photography,
Book design

As part of a module during my bachelor's degree, I was given a fictitious assignment to design a poster for hairdresser Kati Perriard in Zurich. Part of my process was to photograph the hairdressing utensils in the photo studio objectively, subjectively, as a still life and on people. In the design process, I drew on the impressions I had gained in her business premises. Kati's salon is unique in many ways. She displays her collected objects, but they are anything but ordinary. Mostly the objects are combined in some way, for example dresses with a hair collar. The poster picks up on the aspect of combination and artwork and draws attention to what is often forgotten and discarded: the cut hair.

Many thanks to my model Larissa Hoppeler.
