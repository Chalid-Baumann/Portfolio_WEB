Kunstbestand der Stadt Zürich

Photography,
Book design

As part of a module in my bachelor's degree programme, I conceived and designed a book about the art collection of the city of Zurich. Important components are, on the one hand, the predetermined material, but also an individually definable focus topic. This publication is primarily about ‘tangible’ art, about art in public spaces. The aim is to link the artworks with the city and to bring new attention to works that usually receive little attention. The final product is a travel guide through all city districts, primarily for Zurich residents and anyone interested in art. Due to its size, restrictions were imposed and only depictions of women (full-body) in the genres of fountains, sculpture and painting were included. Figures of angels and children were also excluded. This resulted in a selection totalling 105 works. Each work is presented on 2 double pages. The first focuses on a detailed image with a brief description of the title, artist, material and address. Next to this is an initial localisation in the city. The following double page shows further views of the city. The captions explain in which direction the viewer is looking.
