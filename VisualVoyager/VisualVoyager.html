Visual Voyager

Photography,
Programming

As part of a module during my bachelor's degree, I explored exploratory research in both analogue photography and coding and looked for new technical possibilities. I used various plastic bottles and their labels as research material. The website takes the user on a journey. Images and text alternate throughout the landing page. Analogue photography and digital elements complement each other. Elements such as scratches or water create a connection to the analogue photography and serve as a link on the page. The viewer experiences different focal points and interactions that create excitement and offer an experience.
