GIG Powerdrinks and Softdrinks

Corporate Design,
Prototyping,
Photography

The brand GIG plans to enter the Swiss market with a power drink and a new soft drink, targeting also Germany, France, Italy and Austria. For my practical bachelor project, I developed a new visual identity for this purpose, focusing on the packaging design and web presence. The design concept breaks with the stereotypical presentation forms used in the beverage industry in order to stand out from the competition. It features an innovative grid that does away with the usual division into front and back, as well as a minimalist design that emphasises the materiality of the packaging.

Secial thanks to Kevin Waller and Stefan Lugauer from GIG for their appreciated support throughout the process.
