GOODY Packaging

Packaging Design,

Just for myself, I'm working on a mindful packaging line that focuses on creating less waste while still being practical for everyday life. My goal is to design a variaty of products mainly in the beverage and food corner. For a start I focused on three healthy infused fruit boosters, aka "yellow, green and purple bosster", as well as a sour ale line. Really excited to take things further.
