Jagdschloss Wiesen Skateboards

Illustration,
Screen printing,
Photography

My brother has been skateboarding for many years and contacted me with a personal request. He wanted to have a small limited edition of skateboards printed. Together we created and worked out the illustrations, sprayed the unprinted decks white and screen-printed them by hand. This was followed by a photo shoot to show the skateboard in action.

Many thanks to my brother Atilla Baumann.
